About Cookies

Web browser cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information.

Cookies and us

We use cookies to help you navigate or website and technology solutions. We analyse which pages you visit and which features you are using. Cookies are also used to provide features for social media. We may gather server log information such as IP address, browser type and operating system. Such information is used to administrate the website and to enhance the user experience of our services. It is also used to compile statistical reports on website activities

Our Commitment

Data4Life do not collect information for profiling, marketing or advertisement purposes. This means that we do not sell, trade or share such information to any third parties

Your Choice

You as a user may choose to set your web browser to alert you when cookies are being sent or to disallow cookies. Diallowing cookies may affect some of the services provided. Most browsers will allow you to turn off cookies. Please refer to the menu on your browser or to the independent web page www.allaboutcookies.org for more information.

Privacy notice

Data4Life is committed to enabling trust and transparency in the data sharing economy. WithData4Life, we empower people to control the personal data held by organisations. With the GDPR and similar regulations being enforced worldwide, privacy is getting center stage attention.Data4Life facilitates compliance for organisations and provides a convenient tool for you, to know what you have said yes to. This privacy notice describes WHAT INFORMATIONData4Life collects, uses, maintains and shares and for WHAT PURPOSE the personal information is used. We also explain the choices you have regarding the use of your personal data, access to information and your requests for the correction or deletion of such data.

Information we collect

Data4Life collects information when you register with us, subscribe to our newsletter, download our whitepapers or place an order for our products and services. We also collect information when you voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback. You may also visit our website anonymously. We only collect personal information on a voluntary basis. However, withholding consent to use your data may prevent you from accessing certain information, services and activities. Further information on how your data is collected and processed when using theData4Life application and by business partners are as described below.

Personal data protection

Data4Life maintains appropriate standard security practices, including encryption, passwords and physical security measures, as well as management procedures to protect the security and confidentiality of your personal data. Only a limited number of persons within Data4Life AB are authorised to access, modify and delete your personal data that is stored on the www.igrant.io website. In case of any data breach, we will promptly inform our users of it as per the regulatory guideline.

Use and purpose of information

We use your information collected from the www.igrant.io website to personalise your repeat visits to the website. This information helps us respond to your service requests and support your needs more efficiently. We may also use your feedback to improve our products and services, as well as your email address to send information and updates related to your order. Your email address may also be used to respond to inquiries and other requests. We store your email address when you download reports or subscribe to our newsletter and mailing lists.

Access to information and correction

You have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of this information, please email us: our contact details are at the end of the privacy notice. In order to make sure that the personal information we hold about you is accurate and up to date you may ask us to correct or remove any information you think is inaccurate.

Web browser Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log and visitor behaviour information which is used to enhance the user experience, track visitor use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity. You can choose to set your web browser to disallow cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Disallowing cookies may affect some of the services provided by the website. Further information on this item is found under "Cookies Policy" on the igrant.io website.

Marketing

We would like to send you information about our products and services which may be of interest to you. If you have consented to receive marketing information, you may opt out at any time. You also have a right to stop us from contacting you for marketing purposes, at any time. We do not sell to or trade your personal information with others for marketing purposes. You may also request termination of subscription to mailing services (unsubscribe) at any time. We include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email. You may also contact us via email below, see "Contact Information".

Other websites

Our website contains links to other websites and social media sites. This privacy notice only applies to the igrant.io website and services, so you are advised to read the privacy policies of other websites that you link to from our website.

Changes to our privacy policy

We keep our privacy policy under regular review and will place any updates on this web page. The last update on this privacy notice is stated at the bottom of this page.

Contact Information

Please contact our Data Protection Officer by email to dpo@igrant.io if you have any questions about our privacy policy, our website practices and the information we hold about you.
